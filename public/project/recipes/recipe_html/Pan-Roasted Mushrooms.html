Pan-Roasted Mushrooms

   

Tags Vegetable Recipes, Mushrooms
Estimate Time < 1 hour
Cost $

   

Ingredients

1 T. vegetable oil

1.5 lb button or cremini mushrooms, sliced

1 medium shallot, finely minced

2 medium garlic cloves, minced

2 tsp. fresh thyme leaves

2 tsp. soy sauce

1 tsp. fresh lemon juice

2 T. unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large nonstick skillet over high heat. Add mushrooms and cook, tossing, and stirring frequently until they have given up their liquid and the liquid has evaporated, about 9 minutes. Continue cooking until they are a deep brown, about 10 minutes longer.
  2. Add the shallot, garlic, thyme, and toss. Add soy sauce, lemon juice and butter. Turn off heat, season with salt and pepper.
